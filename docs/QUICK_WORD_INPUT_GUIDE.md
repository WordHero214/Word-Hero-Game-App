## üéØ How to Use This Guide

1. **Login as Teacher** to your account
2. **Navigate to Word Bank** tab
3. **Click "Add New Word"** button
4. **Copy the word details** from above
5. **Paste into the form** fields
6. **Select Grade Levels** (e.g., 1-3 for Easy, 4-6 for Hard)
7. **Select Sections** (e.g., A, B, C)
8. **Click "Save Word"**
9. **Repeat** for all 30 words
