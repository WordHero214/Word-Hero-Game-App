# AI Word Generation Prompt for Grade-Level Spelling Words

## Copy and Paste This Prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini

---

**PROMPT START:**

Generate a comprehensive spelling word list for Filipino elementary students (Grades 1-6) in CSV format. Create 30 words per grade level (180 words total), with each word having 10 questions distributed across three difficulty levels.

## Requirements:

### Grade Level Appropriateness:
- **Grade 1**: Simple 3-5 letter words, basic phonics, common objects (cat, dog, sun, red, big)
- **Grade 2**: 4-6 letter words, simple compound words, basic sight words (happy, jump, play, tree, book)
- **Grade 3**: 5-7 letter words, common prefixes/suffixes, everyday vocabulary (beautiful, running, teacher, garden)
- **Grade 4**: 6-9 letter words, more complex patterns, academic vocabulary (important, different, science, history)
- **Grade 5**: 7-10 letter words, advanced patterns, subject-specific terms (photosynthesis, democracy, multiplication)
- **Grade 6**: 8-12 letter words, complex vocabulary, abstract concepts (responsibility, environment, civilization)

### Difficulty Distribution (per grade):
- **10 EASY words**: Basic level for that grade
- **10 MEDIUM words**: Grade-appropriate challenge
- **10 HARD words**: Advanced for that grade level

### CSV Format Required:
```
Word,Difficulty,Grade,Hint (English),Scenario (English),Hint (Filipino),Scenario (Filipino)
```

### Content Guidelines:

1. **Hints (English & Filipino)**:
   - Short, clear definition or description
   - Age-appropriate language
   - Help students understand meaning
   - Example: "A red or green fruit" / "Isang pulang o berdeng prutas"

2. **Scenarios (English & Filipino)**:
   - Complete sentence with blank (use ___)
   - Natural, relatable context
   - Grade-appropriate situations
   - Example: "I ate an ___ for breakfast" / "Kumain ako ng ___ sa almusal"

3. **Filipino Translations**:
   - Use simple, commonly understood Tagalog
   - Match the English meaning closely
   - Keep it natural for Filipino elementary students
   - Use everyday Filipino vocabulary

### Word Selection Criteria:
- Relevant to Filipino student life and culture
- Mix of: objects, actions, descriptions, concepts
- Include words from: home, school, nature, community
- Avoid: offensive words, overly complex terms, rare vocabulary
- Ensure: proper spelling, common usage, educational value
