## üí° Tips for Creating Your Own Words

### Easy Mode Tips
- Use 3-5 letter words
- Choose familiar objects/animals
- Write clear, simple hints
- Use words students see daily

### Medium Mode Tips
- Use 6-10 letter words
- Choose words students can sound out
- Pick words with clear pronunciation
- Avoid words with silent letters

### Hard Mode Tips
- Use 8-15 letter words
- Create meaningful scenarios
- Use academic vocabulary
- Connect to real-world situations
